What We Offer

Start things off by picking a base shade for your necktie. Instead of just one hue, try pairing it with designs like solids, streaks, specks, slanted bands, or grids. Pick another tint just for those shapes so they stand out how you like. Go deeper if needed, adjusting stripe angles - upright, sideways, or tilted across. Then consider where the design shows up: maybe only on the wide part, perhaps all the way through including the loop and upper section too.

Look at your tie with a collar shown by turning it on during preview. Starting the download as PNG gives you the choice - collar in or out. A "Save design (JSON)" button stores everything you set, saving it all inside a file. Come back anytime using that file to match your earlier version exactly.

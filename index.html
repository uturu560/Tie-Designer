About Us

Custom Tie Designer is a free, in-browser tool that lets you design a tie in seconds: choose colours and patterns, see a live preview with an optional shirt collar, and download your design as a PNG or save your settings as JSON.

Our goal is to make it easy for anyone to visualize and share tie designs without leaving the browser—whether for work, events, or personal use.

We are continually improving the tool. If you have ideas or feedback, get in touch at aniebiet350@gmail.com.

FAQ

How do I use the designer?

Pick a tie colour and pattern from the options. You can turn on “Show shirt collar in preview” to see the tie with a collar, and use “Download PNG” to save the image. Use “Include collar in download” to include or exclude the collar in the saved image.

Can I save my design and use it later?

Yes. Use “Save design (JSON)” to download a file with your current settings. You can keep it for reference or use it to recreate the same look by manually matching the colours and pattern in the designer.

Is my design data sent to a server?

No. The designer runs entirely in your browser. Your design choices, downloads, and saved JSON files stay on your device. We do not receive or store your designs.

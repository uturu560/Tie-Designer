About Us

Custom Tie Designer is a simple, in-browser tool to design a tie: choose colors and patterns, see a live preview, and download your design as a PNG or save it as JSON.

Our goal is to make it easy to visualize and share tie designs without leaving the browser.

FAQ

How do I use the designer?

Pick a tie color and pattern, optionally change pattern color and direction in Advanced Settings. Use “Show shirt collar in preview” to see the tie with a collar; use “Download PNG” to save the image.

Can I save my design and load it later?

Yes. Use “Save design (JSON)” to download a file with your current settings. You can re-apply it later by editing the JSON and re-importing if we add a load option, or by manually setting colors and pattern to match.

Is my design data sent to a server?

No. Everything runs in your browser. Downloads and saved JSON stay on your device.

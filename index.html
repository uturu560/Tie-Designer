FAQ

Using the designer?

Start by selecting a hue for your tie from the palette above. Not just one shade - pick another for the design, letting polka dots or lines shine exactly how you like. Instead of guessing, switch on "Show shirt collar in preview" to view it worn naturally. When ready, grab the image using "Download PNG." Want the collar part of that file? Toggle "Include collar in download" either way. Dive into Advanced Settings if direction matters - tilt those stripes vertical, horizontal, maybe even slanted. Patterns might stretch across every section too: knot, tail, middle, all of it.

Can I save my design and use it later?

Sure. Pick "Save design (JSON)" to get a file holding your present setup - colors, pattern, choices. That file stays useful later, if you want to rebuild the exact appearance by hand inside the tool. No automatic upload exists just now. Still, pop the file into any basic text app, find the numbers and names, then apply them where needed. The option to drag it back in might come someday. For now, eyes-on adjustment works fine.

Could your design information be shared with a remote system?

Finding a way through? It is the browser handling all work right where you are. Choices made, things downloaded, files kept as JSON - all remain stored locally, never leaving your machine. What shows up in logs or tracking falls under privacy rules, yet still leaves out every detail of your tie patterns. Nothing sent our way sticks around on our systems.

What file formats do you support?

Your design exports as a PNG picture - include the collar or skip it, that part depends on what you chose earlier. Another option: store your setup using a JSON file instead. Right now, no other image types or design files work here, though new choices could show up later if people ask for them.
